\section{System Model}

\label{sec:sysmodel}

Redundant traffic ratio is defined as:

\begin{equation}
	\rho = \frac{B_T-B}{B}
\end{equation}

Where $B_T$ is the total amount of data downloaded during the playback session and $B$ is the sum of the segments' sizes shown to the user.
